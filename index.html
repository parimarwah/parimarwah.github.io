live coding

my collaborator and i meshed the personal with the internet using the live-coding environment strudel and the console on wikipedia's 'diary' page by taking my diary entries and making them appear on screen while she mixed her voice reading them with music.

the piece mimics the crescendos of a panic attack: entries multiply until the wikipedia page disappears entirely, spinning to depict how off-kilter one feels at the peak and then slowly fades away.
